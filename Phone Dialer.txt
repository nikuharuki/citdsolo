Phone Dialer

Instructions:
program that prompts the user for a word and converts each letter
to the corresponding number, printing the number as many times as needed to meet the letter.


	
1	    2       3
none   ABC     DEF
4       5       6
GHI    JKL     MNO
7       8       9
PQRS   TUV     WXYZ
        0
       none


Sample Input:
Hello

Sample Output:
4433555555666
